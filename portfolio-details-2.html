Paraglider Weather App

Paraglider Weather App, a groundbreaking feature developed exclusively for the LLSC Paragliding Club's website. This innovative application leverages the power of the OpenWeather API to provide detailed weather forecasts for paragliding sites/hills for the next five years. Powered by Python on the backend, the Paraglider Weather App calculates flying conditions for each paragliding site/hill over the next five days, with updates every three hours. Using advanced algorithms and specific criteria such as wind direction, rain, thermal conditions, and more, the app ensures accurate and reliable predictions to enhance safety and enjoyment for paragliders. With its user-friendly interface, comprehensive features, and commitment to safety, the Paraglider Weather App is a must-have tool for paragliders of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned pilot or just starting out, trust the Paraglider Weather App to provide the essential information you need for a successful and enjoyable flight experience.
